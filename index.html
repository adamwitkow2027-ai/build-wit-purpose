Training approach

Better reps, not random reps.

Every session starts with what the athlete actually needs. From there, training moves through targeted teaching, high-quality repetition, and feedback that makes the next rep clearer.

01

Evaluate

Identify current skill needs, movement habits, confidence gaps, and goals.

02

Train

Use focused technical reps, athletic progressions, and simple cues that stick.

03

Apply

Connect technique to game-speed reads, communication, and pressure moments.

Focus areas

Skill work connected to athletic movement.

  • Passing, serve receive, defensive reading, and libero habits
  • Serving mechanics, footwork, platform angles, and controlled reps
  • Jump mechanics, landing control, plyometrics, and change of direction
  • Tryout preparation, confidence, communication, and coachability
